Covid-19

Symptoms

    Most common symptoms are:
  • Fever
  • Dry Cough
  • Sore throat and tiredness

Transmission

    Transmitted by:
  1. Respiratory droplets
  2. Droplets of saliva
  3. Contact Routes
  4. Cough and Sneeze

Prevention

    Covid can be prevented by:
  • Covering your cough and sneeze
  • Washing hands properly and frequently
  • Keeping a safe distance

Prevention is better than cure!!!
